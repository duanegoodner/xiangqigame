Detailed Description

A change in the state of a gameboard::GameBoard represented by a moves::Move, and each of the gamepiece::GamePiece objects located at the start and end locations of the Move.

moves::ExecutedMove.moving_piece is located at moves::ExecutedMove.spaces.start, and moves::ExecutedMove.destication_piece is located at moves::ExecutedMove.spaces.end rior to the change in state.
